# Design Brief

## Style: Neon Arena

The design should evoke the excitement of a packed stadium, with neon lights and bold graphics. The atmosphere should be lively and dynamic, with a focus on community engagement and competition. The visual elements should include futuristic and high-tech details, such as circuit boards and fiber optics, to reflect the blockchain and AR aspects of the app. The overall picture should be one of fast-paced action and thrilling entertainment.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Bright and bold, with a focus on neon blues and purples |
| Typography | Modern and futuristic, with a focus on bold fonts and neon accents |
| Components | Sleek and high-tech, with a focus on metallic and glass-like materials |
| Imagery | Futuristic and dynamic, with a focus on esports and gaming-themed graphics |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** blue
- **Mode:** dark
- **Border Radius:** 0.5

## App Variant: Esports Hub

> Game on with Crypto Stadium

This variant focuses on the esports community, allowing professional gamers to create and trade unique digital collectibles, such as in-game items and exclusive skins. Fans can participate in prediction markets for upcoming matches and engage in AR-powered fantasy leagues. The platform will partner with popular esports teams and leagues to offer exclusive content.

**Target Audience:** Professional esports players and enthusiasts
**Monetization:** Transaction fees on digital collectible trades and prediction market bets
**Complexity:** premium
